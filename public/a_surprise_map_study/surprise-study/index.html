How would you interpret this map?
Before we begin, please read the following consent form carefully and thereafter complete the section below.

Goal: We intend to evaluate how well people interpret a regularized map. Our study evaluates a Bayesian Weighting technique that allows us to show regions of interest on a map.

Procedure: The experiment consists of two tasks. Each task is associated with a map, and you will be required to respond to a task, based on the requirements.

Benefit: You will learn about an interesting technique used to overcome the biases associated with Choropleth Maps.

Duration: The estimated time for the experiment is 7 minutes.

Privacy: Your participation is anonymous and your responses cannot be used to identify you.

Record Keeping: Records of your participation will be held confidential so far as permitted by law. However, the study investigators and, under certain circumstances, the Institutional Review Board will be able to inspect and have access to this data. Any publication or presentation of the data will not identify you.

Reward: $1.40.
